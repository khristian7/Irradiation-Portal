Our nowcasting system generates short-term forecasts (15 minutes to 6 hours ahead) through:

Data Integration

  • Meteorological station data
  • Satellite observations
  • Weather model outputs

AI Models Predict

  • Global Horizontal Irradiance (GHI)
  • Direct Normal Irradiance (DNI)
  • Diffuse Horizontal Irradiance (DHI)

The system analyzes cloud dynamics, atmospheric conditions, and historical patterns to generate high-resolution forecasts updated every 15 minutes.
